GURUDEO PARAMEDICAL COLLEGE works under the Gurudev Educational Trust, which is affiliated by Aryabhatta Knowledge University and Recognized by Health Department of Bihar. The Institute has spent many years in educational field of imparting skill based technical education. The core intent of GURUDEO PARAMEDICAL COLLEGE is to promote Paramedical Health Education sectors in our country. It aims at providing quality education with advanced programs rewarding the students to think in a creative manner and eventually creates a pool of well-trained professionals.

Conveniently located in the (Bihar) which is near to Fatepur Chirimara Kajipur, P.O-Thathan Bujurge,P.S-Hajipur,Vaishali-844125 , the Institute is fully equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, As the programs are diverse, the entire infrastructure is created to cater to the different needs of studying.
